How To Acknowledge Use Of HMFcalc

Thank you very much for using HMFcalc (or the hmf backend) for your research. We hope it has met your needs. If you have found it to be a useful tool, please let us know! The best way to do this is to cite this article, which details HMFcalc's functionality, and also has some background on the HMF.
